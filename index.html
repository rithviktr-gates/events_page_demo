The 2026 Theme
Scaling the Enterprise Channel Across Southeast Asia. What Actually Scales.

A sharper look at how the channel is adapting to AI-led infrastructure, platform-led buying, security-first architecture, and the operational discipline needed to scale across markets.

The Six Themes

Every keynote, panel, and conversation in Bali ladders up to these six questions — the agenda that everyone’s here to work through.

01
Scaling the Enterprise Channel Across Southeast Asia
How the channel is building the capability and discipline required to grow across diverse ASEAN markets.
02
AI Readiness Beyond the Pitch
Real AI opportunity depends on data, infrastructure, governance, and delivery — not just positioning.
03
The Marketplace Shift
Platform-led buying is changing ownership, influence, and how the channel participates in the sales cycle.
04
Security as Core Architecture
Security now sits inside every cloud, AI, and infrastructure decision — not as a separate layer.
05
Replicating Capability Across Markets
What it takes to deliver consistently across countries with different regulatory, talent, and operational realities.
06
Strategy, Brand & IP: Building Beyond Early Traction
Why tech startups need more than product and speed to scale, and how defensible IP turns momentum into sustainable growth.
